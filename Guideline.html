After logging in, browse through the available elections and choose the one you wish to vote in.

Click the "Submit Vote" button to finalize your selection and cast your vote.

Warning:

Once you submit your vote, it cannot be changed. Double-check before confirming.

After the voting period ends, the results will be published for you to view.

Transparency:
