Introduction

eVotix is a mobile based voting platform that allows you to create and manage your own election. eVotix is much more than a platform - it's a revolution. eVotix is what mobile banking is to cash transactions, eVotix is what Whatsapp is to postal department, eVotix is what mobile share trading is to paper shares trading. Today voting means - travelling to a booth, standing there in queue for hours, getting hand inked to vote. evotix lets voter vote directly from their mobile, from the comforts of their homes and that also within few seconds.

Significance of the name - eVotix

The name "Evotix" for an online voting system project can be a great choice for several reasons:

  • "E" for Electronic – It emphasizes that the voting system is digital and electronic.
  • "Vot" from Voting – Clearly represents the purpose of the system.
  • The "ix" could symbolize a sophisticated system or a complex digital framework.
  • Tech-Savvy Sound – The name sounds modern and innovative, which aligns with an online voting system.
  • "Evotix" is short, catchy, and easy to remember, making it a great name for branding.
  • It has a professional and secure ring to it, which is essential for a voting system.

Why Choose Us?

Secure

eVotix's online voting system has many layers of security making the platform 100% secure. Security features include encryption, authentication based on OTP, Aadhaar and face recognisation. Features like voter selfie, double authentication, audit trail and much more.

Easy to Use

eVotix's mobile election system is very user friendly and voting can be done anytime and from anywhere within seconds. Voting can be done via mobile browser, website, android app or iOS app.

Cost Effective

The complete election can be conducted for as low as Rs 10,000. Cost includes the cost of setting up the poll, sending invites, cloud hosting, voting process and also live result counting.
